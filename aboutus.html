Our History

Aum Yoga Studio has been the longest running yoga studio in the city with the best trained teachers in the city.

Our vision is to teach the correct foundations of Yoga and create flexibility and ease within the body and mind. When yoga done in a correct way, it can aleviate many ailments and enhance mood and create a positive mindset.
