Corporate Classes

AUM Yoga Studio also offers classes for Companies and corporates or any group of people who wants to unwind and relax from thier daily stressful life and experience a mindful, relaxed and overal well being.

Occational yoga retreats like these are proven to improve productivity and re-energize Employers as well as improve thier overal health and well being in a fun and enjoyable way.

AUM Yoga Studio is also proud to provide customized programs for such groups depending on the group needs.

How this program works:

  • Employees come together, as a group of any size (depending on your space availability)
  • Classes can be offered any time of day that works for your office;
  • All of our classes are for all ages, fitness levels and body types;
  • Length of classes generally range from 45 min to 90 min;
  • Corporate Programs generally range from 6 to 10 weeks;(minimum 6 weeks)
  • One time classes for special events, conferences, etc. can also be booked;
  • and Yoga mats and equipment can also be arranged !
